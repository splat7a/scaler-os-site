Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 20, 2025

1. Introduction

Scaler-OS ("we," "our," or "us") provides software solutions and services for e-commerce businesses. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our services.

2. Information We Collect and Access

In providing our services, we may collect and access various types of business data:

  • Business account information and credentials
  • E-commerce platform data and analytics
  • Sales and inventory information
  • Advertising campaign performance data
  • Product catalog and listing information

3. Third-Party API Integration

Our services integrate with various third-party platforms and APIs to provide functionality, including but not limited to:

  • Amazon Advertising API (for advertising campaign management and analytics)
  • Amazon Selling Partner API (for seller account data and inventory management)
  • Other e-commerce and business intelligence platforms as needed

We access data from these platforms solely to provide our services and in accordance with each platform's terms of service and data usage policies.

4. How We Use Information

We use the information we access and collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving our software services
  • Campaign management and optimization
  • Performance analysis and reporting
  • Business intelligence and strategic insights
  • Inventory and catalog management
  • Technical support and customer service

5. Data Protection and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the data we access and process. We comply with applicable data protection laws and the terms of service of all third-party platforms we integrate with. We do not collect, store, or share personally identifiable information from end consumers.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your business data with third parties except:

  • As necessary to provide our services (e.g., cloud hosting providers)
  • When required by law or legal process
  • With your explicit consent

7. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time, subject to legal and contractual requirements.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about your business
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke access to third-party platform integrations
